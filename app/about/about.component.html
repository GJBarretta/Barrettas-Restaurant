About Us

Since opening in February of 2002, Barretta's Italian Restaurant has been a staple in the Kennett Square community.

Lunch and Dinner is served daily with a wide array of sandwiches, paninis, soups, pizza, entrees and salads using the finest ingredients. Barretta's offers daily lunch specials and lunch combos including subs, cheese steaks, calzones, stromboli using imported Italian meats and cheeses.

Domenico and Giovanna Barretta have assembled a collection of homemade Italian meals including Pasta Primavera, Alfredo and their own Pasta Barretta. Steak, Veal, Chicken and Seafood along with Baked Pasta Dishes available daily.

Most major credit cards are accepted. Barretta's is a BYOB and smoke free restaurant.
